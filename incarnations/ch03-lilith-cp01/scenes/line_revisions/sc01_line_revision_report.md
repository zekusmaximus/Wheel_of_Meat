## Executive Summary

This 46-line opening scene for Chapter 3 is **ambitious and largely successful**—the temporal integration technique (Lilith's present intertwining with Ka's prehistory) is executed with genuine craft, and the voice shows strong sensory anchoring. ## 6. Priority Line Revisions

### LINE 7: Technical + Loaded Gesture

**Current:**
> "Lilith reached for the handle—fingers closing around plastic warmth, around the smooth cold of a bone-knife hilt—and poured water over leaves that would not exist for epochs, watching them bloom into color the way blood bloomed in snow when prey fell wrong."

**Problem:**
- Double em-dash creates parsing difficulty
- "watching them bloom" — "them" slightly ambiguous after multiple entities

**Suggested Revision:**
> "Lilith reached for the handle—fingers closing around plastic warmth, around the smooth cold of a bone-knife hilt—and poured water over leaves that would not exist for epochs, watching the leaves bloom into color the way blood bloomed in snow when prey fell wrong."

**Rationale:** Keeps the powerful dual-dash construction (it's doing temporal overlay work) but clarifies antecedent.

### LINE 25: Voice + Loaded Gesture

**Current:**
> "The thread she had found was old in a way that made her own iterations seem recent, old in a way that made the Alexandria physician feel like yesterday. It receded into temporal depths she could not calculate, folding backward through time like a river viewed from its delta, its source hidden in distances that made her chest tighten with something she would not call recognition."

**Problem:**
- Body reaction ("chest tighten") comes at end—inverted sensory anchoring
- No physical action during the reaching

**Suggested Revision:**

Her fingers stilled around the mug as the thread receded into temporal depths she could not calculate, folding backward through time like a river viewed from its delta, its source hidden in distances that tightened her chest with something she would not call recognition."

**Rationale:** Adds loaded gesture (fingers stilling = attention capture) and front-loads physical reaction for sensory anchoring compliance.

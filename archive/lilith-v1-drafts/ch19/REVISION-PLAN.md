## STAGE 2: HIGH PRIORITY REVISIONS (STRONGLY RECOMMENDED)

### REVISION 4: Add "Together Doesn't Hold" Callback

**Priority:** HIGH
**Estimated Time:** 15 minutes
**Location:** Scene 9, around line 820

#### What's Needed

Explicit reference to the phrase from Ch7/Ch13 explaining one-per-system rule

#### Where to Insert

**Current text (Scene 9, lines 819-828):**
```
She understood now what she hadn't understood at twenty-five, when she'd first glimpsed
a dying man in ancient waters and assumed she could simply redirect his path. The soul
she was tracking wasn't just resilient. It wasn't just clever. It had some quality—or
perhaps the path itself had some quality—that made obstruction indistinguishable from
support.

When she pushed, he grew.

When she obstructed, he strengthened.
```

#### Suggested Addition (After line 828)

```
And still she couldn't stop. Couldn't let him finish first. Together doesn't hold—
she'd observed this across her own past lives, the pattern of near-completion
interrupted when another presence approached the same threshold. The path admitted
one being at a time. If he crossed first, she would cycle again. Eons of work,
lost.

So what, she wrote slowly, if I stopped pushing?
```

#### Rationale

- References the key phrase from earlier chapters
- Explains her understanding (which is correct about structure, wrong about meaning)
- Shows why she can't give up
- Sets up the tragic irony (together actually means merger)

### REVISION 5: Add Face Constancy Observation

**Priority:** HIGH
**Estimated Time:** 15 minutes
**Location:** Scene 1 or Scene 7

#### Option A: Scene 1 (Preferred)

**Where:** After line 50, during tracking methodology explanation

**Suggested Addition:**
```
Her own face in the tracking—when she glimpsed her past incarnations in the peripheral
awareness that sometimes accompanied deep observation—was always the same. The widow's
peak. The dark strand falling left. The particular way light caught the crescent in

His face changed every time.

She had never wondered why. The observation filed itself away, a detail that seemed

```

#### Option B: Scene 7 (Alternative)

**Where:** During the montage of tracking multiple lives (around line 545-565)

**Suggested Addition:**
```
Tracking through the lives that followed—a Japanese factory worker, brief; an American
child who died young; the detective who would occupy most of the middle century—she
noted again the changing faces. Different every time. Where her own face remained
constant across every glimpse of her past, his shifted: different ethnicities,
different ages, different presentations entirely.

The pattern meant something. She didn't know what.
```

#### Rationale

- Plants the mystery early
- Foreshadows Maitreya (same face = same role; changing face = learning through variation)
- Visible to reader, invisible to Lilith
- Quick and subtle

### REVISION 6: Strengthen "No Intervention" Decision in Scene 7

**Priority:** HIGH
**Estimated Time:** 30 minutes
**Location:** Scene 7, around line 570-580

#### Current Text (Lines 571-581)

```
She didn't intervene.

She could have. The power was there. She could have manifested as a client—the genre
practically required it—could have attached to him, could have engineered
ever-more-spectacular failures. She could have tested his perseverance the way she'd
tested Wei's determination.

failure would teach him to fail better.*
```

#### Issue

The decision is present but could be clearer about WHY she's learned this lesson

#### Suggested Expansion

**Replace lines 577-581 with:**
```
But she knew now how that would end.

Eight incarnations. Eight interventions. Eight backfires—the list was complete now,
Wei's purified determination the final proof. The pattern was undeniable. Distance
didn't prevent it. Method didn't prevent it. The only constant was her opposition—
and every opposition accelerated rather than obstructed.

I would refine him, she thought. Every obstacle would become a lesson. Every failure
would teach him to fail better. Even here, even in this marginal life, my interference
would polish rather than corrupt.

So she simply watched. The detective died in an alley, his final case unfinished but
still in his mind. Quick violence, the kind that films glamorized and reality made
ugly.
```

#### Rationale

- Makes the learning explicit
- Shows the pattern recognition
- Demonstrates growth (she's adapting her strategy)
- Strengthens the logic of pivoting to intimacy

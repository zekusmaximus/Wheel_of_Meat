## I. FALLING-IN-LOVE SCENES (7,500 words)

**Editor Issue #3:** "We're told they had dinner at The Walnut, wine at her flat, bedroom confessions. We never see these scenes."

### FL-1: The Walnut Restaurant — 2,500 words

**Setting:** The Walnut Persian Restaurant, Manchester, evening. Early relationship (2-3 weeks after meeting).

**Function:** Establish Silas as someone who desires, not just observes.

**Beats:**
1. **Arrival** (200w): Silas nervous; Lilith composed, watching
2. **First vulnerability** (400w): Silas orders in hesitant Farsi; her masked interest
3. **Philosophical sparring** (500w): Consciousness debate—his idealism, her materialism
4. **Crack in her armor** (400w): She mentions "a long time ago" (seed for later)
5. **Physical attraction** (400w): Hands touch reaching for bread; neither pulls away
6. **Silas's want crystallized** (300w): He wants her secrets, not just arguments
7. **Exit** (300w): Walking into Manchester night, both changed

**Sensory Anchors:**
- Smell of saffron and lamb
- Persian tile pattern on wall
- Rain on windows
- Her silver bracelet

**Integration:** Chapter 1, as flashback triggered by current crisis. Slots between lines 50-100 of current Ch 1.

**Editor Issues Addressed:** #2 (Silas wants), #3 (relationship shown)

### FL-2: Lilith's Flat, Wine — 3,000 words

**Setting:** Lilith's flat, late evening. Two weeks after The Walnut.

**Function:** Show physical/emotional intimacy + Lilith's genuine conflict.

**Beats:**
1. **Comfort established** (300w): Routines visible—she opens wine, he finds his spot
2. **Philosophical pillow talk** (500w): Identity and continuity of self
3. **Lilith's slip** (400w): References "before I was born" then covers
4. **Silas's attraction to mystery** (400w): Drawn in by her strangeness
5. **First real kiss** (500w): Initiated by Silas (AGENCY)
6. **Post-kiss silence** (400w): Lilith's something-like-fear—genuine vulnerability
7. **Deflection** (500w): She turns to philosophy, but something shifted

**Sensory Anchors:**
- Wine (red, particular vintage she mentions)
- Her bookshelves (one wall)
- City lights through window
- Her bare feet on hardwood

**Echo in Manchester:** Later, Silas returns to this flat during crisis—same view, different meaning.

**Integration:** Chapter 1, as extended flashback. Follows FL-1 with brief transition.

**Editor Issues Addressed:** #2 (Silas acts), #3 (intimacy shown)

### FL-3: Campus Walk, Morning — 2,000 words

**Setting:** University of Manchester campus, autumn morning. A month into relationship.

**Function:** Establish ordinary life that awakening will threaten.

**Beats:**
1. **Domesticity** (300w): Walking naturally, finishing each other's thoughts
2. **Stewart foreshadowing** (400w): Discussion of troubled student
3. **Silas's contentment** (300w): Realizes he's happy—surprise
4. **Lilith's wistfulness** (400w): She looks at him like memorizing a face
5. **Small conflict** (400w): Disagreement about handling the student
6. **Reconciliation** (200w): They agree to disagree—novel intimacy for him

**Sensory Anchors:**
- Coffee cups (specific coffee shop)
- Autumn leaves
- Her coat collar turned up
- His worn tweed

**Integration:** Chapter 1, morning scene between flashbacks and current crisis.

**Editor Issues Addressed:** #2 (stakes visible), #3 (routine shown)

## II. STEWART RESOLUTION SCENES (4,500 words)

**Editor Issue #2, #3:** "He falls from the rooftop (ambiguously), then essentially disappears from the narrative."

### SR-1: Hospital Visit — 2,500 words

**Setting:** North Manchester General Hospital, psychiatric ward. Three days after rooftop.

**Function:** Dramatize consequence; show Silas's guilt and complicity.

**Beats:**
1. **Approach** (300w): Silas hesitates at ward entrance—guilt, uncertainty
2. **Permission** (200w): Allowed in as "the professor who helped"—ironic
3. **First sight of Stewart** (400w): Sedated, diminished, nothing like prophet
4. **Conversation** (500w): Stewart wakes, asks "Did you see it too?"
5. **Silas's honesty** (400w): Admits he saw something—first voicing
6. **Stewart's fear** (400w): "I don't want to see it again. It was too empty."
7. **Lilith appears** (300w): She's monitoring Stewart—tension

**Sensory Anchors:**
- Fluorescent lights
- Antiseptic smell
- Wristband on Stewart's arm
- Beeping monitors

**Emotional Beat:** Silas confronts: his teaching triggered this. Different experiences (liberation vs. terror).

**Integration:** Chapter 22, near beginning. Establishes stakes for Silas's own struggle.

**Editor Issues Addressed:** #2 (guilt), #3 (Stewart resolved)

### SR-2: Stewart's Departure — 2,000 words

**Setting:** University counseling center, then campus exit. Two weeks after rooftop.

**Function:** Complete Stewart arc; force Silas to confront his own experience.

**Beats:**
1. **Final meeting** (300w): Stewart taking medical leave
2. **Changed** (300w): Medicated, stable, fundamentally different
3. **Gratitude and accusation** (400w): Thanks for catch, blame for vision
4. **Silas accepts** (300w): Takes blame without defending
5. **Stewart's insight** (400w): "You're different too. What happened to you?"
6. **Silas's deflection** (300w): "Just shock"—first lie to himself

**Sensory Anchors:**
- Stewart's packed bag
- Forms for medical leave
- Silas's reflection in window
- Outside: students walking, oblivious

**Emotional Beat:** Hollow mantra: "I am boundless awareness" now sounds like denial.

**Integration:** Chapter 22, mid-chapter. Before breakthrough moments.

**Editor Issues Addressed:** #2 (resistance shown), #3 (Stewart complete)

## III. SILAS RESISTANCE SCENES (4,500 words)

**Editor Issue #2:** "Show him suppressing visions. Show him clinging to normalcy."

### RC-1: Ellen Therapy Session — 2,500 words

**Setting:** Ellen's office, university counseling. A week after Stewart crisis.

**Function:** Show Silas choosing denial; show Lilith's network of control.

**Beats:**
1. **Lilith's referral** (200w): She insisted—managing him
2. **Ellen's competence** (300w): Good therapist; sees something's off
3. **Silas deflects** (400w): Discusses Stewart, avoids himself
4. **Ellen probes** (400w): "But what about you? What did you see?"
5. **First admission** (400w): Mentions "flashes"—frames as stress
6. **Ellen's interpretation** (400w): "Dissociation under trauma"—he embraces this
7. **Lilith's technique** (200w): Ellen recommends the meditation—irony
8. **Exit** (200w): Feels contained, controlled—but wrong

**Sensory Anchors:**
- Ellen's neutral office
- Tissue box (never used)
- Silas's hands gripping armrests
- Clock ticking

**Dramatic Irony:** Reader knows what Silas is suppressing.

**Integration:** Chapter 1 (expanded), after FL scenes but before current crisis.

**Editor Issues Addressed:** #2 (resistance), #3 (Ellen visible)

**Sensory Anchors:**

**Integration:** Chapter 1, after RC-1, building toward car scene.

## IV. MANCHESTER ANCHOR SCENES (4,000 words)

**Editor Issue #3:** "Ground us back in Manchester more frequently during the middle third."

### Design Principle

Each anchor is 750-1,000 words, positioned after a historical dive to return reader to present-day stakes.

### BT-1: After Chandra (Post-Betrayal) — 1,000 words

**Setting:** Manchester campus, morning after vision of Chandra's imprisonment.

**Beats:**
- Silas at coffee shop, exhausted
- Colleague notices he looks unwell
- He deflects—"work stress"
- Glimpse of someone who looks like Devaka (random student)—freezes
- Returns to "normal" but cracks showing

**Purpose:** First sign that past lives are bleeding through.

**Integration:** After Chandra chapter (compressed); before Diego chapter.

### BT-2: After Diego (Post-Inquisition) — 1,000 words

**Setting:** Lilith's flat. Silas confides—but she's steering.

**Beats:**
- Admits strange dreams
- Lilith probes carefully—gathering intelligence
- Offers new meditation exercise—another binding technique
- Silas trusts her; dramatic irony

**Purpose:** Show Lilith's manipulation in real-time.

**Integration:** After Diego chapter (compressed); before Renaissance chapter.

### BT-3: After Renaissance — 750 words

**Setting:** Faculty meeting. Brief tension.

**Beats:**
- Routine meeting; Silas distracted
- Lilith watches closely
- He zones out—flash of Renaissance skeptic's argument
- Snaps back; only Lilith notices
- After: her touch, concerned or controlling?

**Purpose:** Show visions intruding on professional life.

**Integration:** After Renaissance chapter; before Taiping chapter.

### BT-4: After Malone — 1,250 words

**Setting:** Hospital—checking Stewart's status; then Silas alone.

**Beats:**
- Calls hospital; Stewart discharged
- Relief mixed with guilt
- Looks at Chandogya Upanishad on desk
- Almost throws it away; hides in drawer instead
- Another act of suppression

**Purpose:** Connect noir death to contemporary stakes; show suppression deepening.

**Integration:** After Malone chapter; before Manchester breakthrough.
